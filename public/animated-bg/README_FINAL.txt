Final animated background (all features)
Files:
 - final.css  (main styles)
 - final.js   (main logic: butterflies, parallax, UI)
Usage:
 - Click the 'BG' button at bottom-right to open settings.
 - Toggle Day/Night, density, performance mode, intensity, and ambient tone.
Accessibility:
 - Add class `reduce-neon` to <body> to lower visual intensity.
 - Add class `performance-mode` to <body> to reduce FPS and visual load.
